INDRAYANI RICE (WHITE)

Discription

Ambemohar is low-yielding compared to other varieties of rice, primarily because it is susceptible to diseases. The hybrid called Indrayani with Ambe Mohar parentage was released in 1987. It was developed by Rice Research Centre near Lonavala. Today, Indrayani has replaced Ambemohar in almost all paddy fields in the area.

Basmati Rice (Brown)

Discription

Brown Basmati Rice is a naturally aromatic whole-grain rice grown in Pakistan. With a fluffy cooked texture and nutty, earthy flavor and aroma, this fragrant rice has its nutritious bran layer intact, giving it higher fiber, firmer texture and stronger flavor than white basmati rice.
